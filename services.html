If we decide to work together: The Ritual Reset

After our free call, if the Ritual Reset feels like a fit, here's what happens.

1. Clarify your current priority

We'll identify what matters most to you right now and use that focus to shape your ritual. Your priority might be:

  • Physical wellness
  • Mental and emotional balance
  • Productivity and focus
  • Financial grounding
  • Reflection and spiritual practice
  • Relationships and connection
  • Creativity and joy
  • Home environment or travel rhythms

2. Co-design your custom wellness ritual

Together we'll shape a habit or routine that fits your real life and moves you forward. This might look like:

  • A no-scroll morning grounding to start your day without spiraling
  • A wind-down that actually helps your nervous system rest
  • A weekly check-in for groceries, finances, or planning
  • Protected creative blocks for writing, art, or flow work
  • Gentle movement nudges that fit your actual energy
  • Meal and hydration anchors so essentials don't get skipped

3. Customize your phone to support your ritual

We'll apply intentional design changes to reduce noise and support your new rhythm. Depending on your goals, I'll guide you through:

  • Focus Modes that shift with your time, place, or energy
  • Home screen layouts that center what matters and hide what doesn't
  • Automations to create seamless starts, stops, or transitions
  • Shortcuts for routines, boundaries, or intention resets
  • Widgets that gently guide your attention where you want it
  • Quiet cues like filtered notifications and hidden apps to protect your space
