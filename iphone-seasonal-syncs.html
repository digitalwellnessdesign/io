iPhone Seasonal Syncs

A year-long coaching journey with seasonal redesigns to keep your screen — and your life — aligned with your evolving priorities.

Coming this fall! Join my email list for updates and early access

There's a reason New Year's goals don't stick

They're built on urgency, not rhythm. But nature knows better. It teaches us to bloom, pause, harvest, and rest.

Seasonal Syncs are a guided year-long journey through the real seasons of your life — Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter — supported by intentional phone rituals and digital flow.

We begin with a grounding intro session to set your focus, then check in at the turn of each season to reset, reflect, and realign.

Includes

  • 1 60-Minute Intro Session to clarify your wellness goals, daily rhythm, and current iPhone habits
  • 4 60-Minute Seasonal Sessions (spring, summer, fall, winter) to get clear on your current focus and wellness priorities
  • Seasonal reflection before each session
  • Custom iPhone redesigns (mockups and instructions to make changes on your own phone) + rituals aligned with the season's energy
  • Post-session guides with simple next steps and nature-aligned practices

This is for you if...

  • You feel your best when you move with nature, not against it
  • You want your phone and calendar to support, not scatter, your energy
  • You're creating, healing, or shifting into a new version of yourself
  • You crave intentional structure without rigidity

Why the seasonal focus? Slow and steady growth over time

Nature gives us permission to evolve. With each session, you're not starting over — you're building momentum rooted in seasonal wisdom and self-trust. This allows for sustainable wellness grounded in who you are now.
