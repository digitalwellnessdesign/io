What's included

Seasonal Reflection

We begin by reflecting on where you are in both the outer season and your own inner cycle. This helps set the tone for the redesign by clarifying what matters now, what you're growing toward, and how your tech can support that shift.

Optional astrology bonus: if you provide your birthdate, time and place, I'll share insights from vedic astrology focused on timing.

Daily Rhythm Mapping

We'll use a Notion tool, the Daily Rhythm Mapper, so your intentions and redesign are rooted in your actual day-to-day. This tool helps us see your natural energy patterns plus your focus windows and transition points. It helps reveal where in your day to add digital support for your goals.

Full iPhone Redesign

We'll co-create a custom, supportive digital environment across:

  • Home Screen(s)

  • Lock Screen
  • Focus Modes
  • Screen Limits
  • Automations + reminders

All tailored to your nervous system, your goals, and your current capacity.

Visual Redesign Mockups + Implementation Time

We'll walkthrough how to make changes to your iPhone step by step so you'll leave the call with newly redesigned screens. You'll also receive a visual example showing your redesigned screens as a reference. Plus, it includes instructions to set up any focus modes, limits and automations.
