2. Co-design your custom wellness ritual

Together we'll shape a habit or routine that fits your real life and moves you forward.

  • No-scroll morning grounding – start your day without spiraling into feeds
  • Wind-down that works – a cue that helps your nervous system actually rest
  • Weekly check-in – for groceries, finances or other planning
  • Protected creative blocks – focused time for writing, art or flow work

We'll apply intentional design changes to reduce noise and support your new rhythm. Depending on your goals, I'll guide you through some combination of these options:

  • Automations to create seamless starts, stops, or transitions
  • Shortcuts for routines, boundaries, or intention resets
  • Widgets that gently guide your attention where you want it
  • Quiet cues like filtered notifications and hidden apps to protect your space
