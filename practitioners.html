For Wellness Practitioners

Help your clients make lifestyle changes stick with technology that supports their wellness plans.

You know what your clients need. Digital wellness helps them actually do it.

As a wellness practitioner, you guide your clients toward healthier habits — better sleep, consistent movement, stress reduction, mindful eating, or daily rituals that support their goals.

But here's the challenge: they reach for their phone 150+ times a day, and often that same device becomes the biggest barrier to following through.

That's where digital wellness design comes in.

A complementary service for your practice

Digital wellness design isn't a replacement for your work — it's a supportive layer that helps your recommendations land and stick.

For nutritionists and health coaches

Your client wants to meal prep on Sundays, drink more water, or avoid late-night snacking. Their iPhone can send gentle reminders, block distracting apps during meal times, and create focus modes that support their eating windows.

For therapists and mental health practitioners

Your client is working on setting boundaries, grounding practices, or reducing anxious scrolling. Their phone can be redesigned to support morning journaling routines, limit social media access during vulnerable hours, and automate calming transitions between work and rest.

For yoga teachers and movement specialists

Your client wants to stretch daily, take walking breaks, or create a consistent practice. Their iPhone can prompt movement at natural energy dips, hide distractions during practice time, and make it easier to choose grounding over scrolling.

For sleep coaches and nervous system specialists

Your client needs to wind down earlier, reduce blue light exposure, and create evening rituals. Their phone can automate red light filters at sunset, silence notifications after a certain hour, and offer gentle cues that support rest instead of stimulation.

Why refer clients to digital wellness design?

  • Your clients get personalized tech support that reinforces your recommendations
  • You don't have to become a tech expert — that's my role
  • Clients see faster progress when their environment (including their phone) supports their goals
  • This creates a seamless extension of your care without adding to your workload
